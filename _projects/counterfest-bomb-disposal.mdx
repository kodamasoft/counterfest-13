# COUNTERFEST BOMB DISPOSAL

I WANNA SEE IT GO OFF! We at COUNTERFEST are setting up a BOMB DISPOSAL for chiptunes that are too dangerous for others to handle! COUNTERFEST BOMB DISPOSAL is a compilation album specially made for chiptune artists that is free of rules and expectations. Put on a blindfold and bring out your bats, it’s time to make art.

<WrappableParagraph title="Presentation">
    BOMB DISPOSAL aims to showcase experimental works of chiptune. We’re looking for tracks that explore an aspect of the medium that you personally are not familiar with, whether that be genres, software/hardware, techniques, or the entirety of chiptune for those of you who are new to it. We aren’t looking to push the limits of the medium; we’re looking to push your own.
</WrappableParagraph>

<WrappableParagraph title="COUNTERFEST BOMB DISPOSAL Specific Music Rules">

COUNTERFEST BOMB DISPOSAL is at its heart a chiptune compilation album, and therefore the main requirement for any tracks submitted is for it to qualify as chiptune.

- We do not have any restrictions regarding software or hardware used, but songs submitted to this compilation should predominantly feature sound synthesis techniques associated with chiptune (e.g. PSG, FM, wavetable). So, for example, an otherwise unrelated song which includes a square wave instrument would not be a fit for this album.

- Songs can be original, or arrangements and remixes based on an existing work.

- As the concept of this album focuses on your personal outlook and experimentation, we would like to hear from you on the thought process behind your track along with the track submission.

- When submitting your final mix, please include stems of your track if possible. If the software you are using to create your track supports exporting to formats such as VGM or NSF, please include that export with your final mix instead. These will be used to create an oscilloscope view of your track for use in the video crossfade.
